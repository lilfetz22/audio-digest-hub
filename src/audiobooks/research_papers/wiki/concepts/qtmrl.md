## TLDR

A modular, dynamic framework designed to replace rigid statistical models with self-correcting agents.

## Body

QTMRL is presented as a framework for enterprise agentic systems that balances flexibility with structural integrity. Unlike traditional statistical models that rely on fixed parameters and predefined outcomes, QTMRL enables agents to adapt to changing conditions in real-time.

The framework is modular, allowing developers to integrate it into existing workflows without completely overhauling their architecture. By shifting toward self-correcting agents, QTMRL aims to reduce manual oversight in complex decision-making tasks by allowing the agent to evaluate its own output against established logic or environmental feedback.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

While the framework promises self-correction, it may introduce 'black box' behaviors where the underlying logic of the correction is difficult to audit or predict, potentially creating new compliance challenges. Furthermore, the efficiency of these self-correcting loops depends heavily on the quality of the feedback mechanisms implemented.
