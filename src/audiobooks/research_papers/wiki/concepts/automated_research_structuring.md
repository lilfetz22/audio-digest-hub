## TLDR

Complex research drafting can be reliably automated by breaking tasks into multi-stage workflows with specialized agents and strict evidence requirements.

## Body

The concept highlights that while AI cannot yet guarantee absolute scientific truth (often referred to as "autonomous science"), the structural process of drafting research is highly automatable. By orchestrating a system of specialized agents—such as utilizing 23 distinct agents across 30 graph nodes—complex, high-level goals can be mapped into executable, step-by-step workflows.

A critical component of this automation is the use of "hard blockers." These are strict systemic requirements, such as mandating traceable evidence for every generated claim. This ensures that the AI does not simply hallucinate plausible text but instead builds a structurally sound and logically connected draft.

The ultimate output of this structured automation is a comprehensive document that is primed for human review. By automating the structural heavy lifting, human researchers are elevated from the drudgery of formatting and initial synthesis, allowing them to focus entirely on the final, critical scientific evaluation.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

The system fundamentally cannot guarantee scientific truth or validity; it only ensures structural soundness and traceability. It still heavily relies on human experts for the final critical evaluation and verification of the underlying scientific claims.
