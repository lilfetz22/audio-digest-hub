## TLDR

A foundational, domain-agnostic framework designed to facilitate agentic science through iterative evolution rather than static task execution.

## Body

EvoMaster is proposed as a solution to the limitations of 'single-pass' AI agents. Unlike typical frameworks that treat each task as an isolated event, EvoMaster implements a system architecture that emphasizes the iterative loop of scientific research: hypothesis generation, experimental execution, critique, and refinement.

By making 'evolution' a core architectural component, EvoMaster aims to enable agents to learn from trial and error across various scientific domains. This approach moves away from domain-specific silos, providing a unified harness that allows research agents to maintain context and improve their performance over extended project timelines.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

The requirement for a 'foundational' framework across diverse scientific fields poses significant integration challenges, as domain-specific knowledge often necessitates highly specialized tools and data structures. Furthermore, the computational cost of maintaining and evolving such a broad agent framework may be prohibitive for smaller research institutions.
