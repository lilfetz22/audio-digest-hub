## TLDR

A mixed-initiative workspace designed to bridge the gap between rapid AI-generated prototyping and maintainable Visual Analytics application development.

## Body

BONSAI addresses the 'vibe coding' paradigm by transitioning from treatable AI outputs as final artifacts to treating them as managed processes. It serves as an environment where developers and AI agents collaborate, specifically targeting the complexities of Visual Analytics (VA) where data pipelines, ML models, and front-end components intersect.

By representing architectural constraints as machine-readable seeds, BONSAI forces AI agents to operate within defined boundaries. This structure prevents the 'tangled mess' of code typically generated by LLMs by enforcing modularity and oversight throughout the development lifecycle.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

The system assumes a level of developer proficiency required to define 'machine-readable seeds' that may create a higher barrier to entry for non-technical users. Additionally, enforcing strict constraints could potentially stifle the creative or emergent architectural solutions that AI agents occasionally produce during unconstrained prototyping.
