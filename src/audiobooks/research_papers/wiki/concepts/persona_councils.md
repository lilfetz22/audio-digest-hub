## TLDR

A multi-agent framework where distinct AI personas debate research plans to prevent bland consensus.

## Body

Persona Councils replace a single monolithic AI agent with a group of specialized agents. In the described system, the council consists of three distinct roles: the *Practical Compass*, the *Rigor & Novelty* expert, and the *Narrative Architect*.

These agents engage in structured debating rounds regarding the research plan. By forcing agents with different priorities to argue and defend their viewpoints, the system avoids the "bland consensus" trap—a common failure mode where a single LLM defaults to the most average, unoriginal, or agreeable output.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

Multi-agent debates can be computationally expensive and time-consuming. There is also a risk of infinite loops or failure to reach an actionable consensus if the personas are too adversarial.
