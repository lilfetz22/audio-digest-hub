## TLDR

A methodology that shifts AI interaction from open-ended chat to the structured management of distinct research artifacts using contracts and multi-agent councils.

## Body

Artifact-Centric Workflow Management is the core methodological innovation of the pAI/MSc framework. Instead of relying on open-ended conversational interfaces (i.e., 'chatting with an LLM'), the system focuses on the systematic generation, validation, and refinement of specific research artifacts.

This approach utilizes 'Artifact Contracts' and 'Multi-Agent Councils' to structure the research workflow. By treating components like literature reviews, proof sketches, and experimental code as distinct, contract-governed deliverables rather than conversational outputs, the system can systematically manage the research pipeline. This structured approach is what allows the framework to significantly reduce the need for continuous human micro-management.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

Rigidly defining the research process as a set of predefined artifact contracts may stifle the organic, non-linear pivots that often occur during genuine scientific discovery. Furthermore, the efficacy of 'Multi-Agent Councils' relies heavily on the individual agents' abilities to evaluate complex mathematics and logic, an area where current LLMs still frequently hallucinate or fail.
