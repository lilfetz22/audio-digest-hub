## TLDR

A new AI framework designed to automate the machine learning research process and drastically reduce the administrative and cognitive overhead of creating submission-ready manuscripts.

## Body

The pAI/MSc framework is proposed as a solution to the extensive cognitive and administrative overhead involved in machine learning research. It aims to streamline the complex transition from a raw research hypothesis to a rigorous, submission-ready academic manuscript.

Unlike previous agentic systems that require heavy human oversight, pAI/MSc seeks to drastically compress the number of human interventions needed. The framework targets a reduction from thousands of tokens of interaction to approximately 10 meaningful human interventions, ensuring that mathematical and academic rigor is maintained without the researcher having to micromanage the AI agent.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

While the framework aims to reduce human intervention to around 10 interactions, it remains to be seen if this low level of oversight can consistently produce novel, mathematically sound, and peer-review-quality research across highly diverse or niche ML domains. There is a risk that minimizing oversight might lead to hallucinated proofs, derivative research, or a loss of deep scientific intuition.
