## TLDR

CoAuthorAI is a system that constrains LLMs within an expert-defined framework using vector-based verification to engineer high-quality, precise documents.

## Body

CoAuthorAI represents a paradigm shift from conversational AI to structured document engineering. By constraining Large Language Models (LLMs) within a rigid, expert-defined framework, the system ensures that the generated content adheres strictly to specific scientific or professional standards rather than relying on open-ended generation.

A key mechanism of CoAuthorAI is its use of vector-based verification. This technique actively checks the model's outputs against verified knowledge, minimizing hallucinations and ensuring the precision required for rigorous scientific writing and complex human workflows.

Ultimately, CoAuthorAI demonstrates how AI agents can be effectively utilized to scale complex, high-quality human work. By enforcing strict guardrails, it maintains the integrity and accuracy demanded by scientific disciplines while accelerating the writing and research process.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

While highly precise, rigid expert-defined frameworks can stifle the creative or emergent capabilities of LLMs, potentially limiting novel scientific synthesis. Furthermore, vector-based verification is only as reliable as the underlying vector database; gaps, inaccuracies, or biases in the reference data will inherently limit the system's overall accuracy.
