## TLDR

Using AI agents to invent and iterate on the underlying logic and architecture of solvers rather than just optimizing parameters.

## Body

AI-driven architecture generation represents a paradigm shift from traditional AI optimization. Instead of using artificial intelligence merely to tune the parameters of a pre-existing function or model, AI agents are tasked with inventing and iterating upon the foundational logic and architecture of the systems themselves.

This approach is particularly impactful for optimization and time-series tasks. By directing an agent to iterate on the underlying logic of a solver, developers can discover novel architectures and algorithmic approaches that human engineers might not have conceived, paving the way for systems that autonomously design their own foundational software.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

Allowing AI to invent underlying logic can result in overly complex, uninterpretable, or computationally inefficient architectures. Validating, verifying, and maintaining AI-generated solvers remains a significant challenge, as the resulting logic may be a 'black box' that performs well in testing but fails unpredictably in real-world edge cases.
