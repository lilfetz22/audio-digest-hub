## TLDR

A mathematical optimization technique used to calculate dynamically feasible spacecraft trajectories by linearizing non-convex constraints.

## Body

Sequential Convex Programming is the 'safety gatekeeper' within the proposed architecture. In space operations, trajectory optimization often involves non-linear dynamics and complex safety constraints, such as keeping a specific distance from other satellites. SCP approximates these difficult problems as a series of convex sub-problems that can be solved efficiently.

By serving as the final stage of the pipeline, the SCP solver ensures that the waypoints generated by the neural network are physically attainable. If a requested behavior violates safety boundaries or fuel constraints, the SCP solver re-calculates the trajectory to maintain physical feasibility, acting as a safeguard against unsafe autonomous decisions.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

SCP is sensitive to the initial guess provided to the solver, which can lead to convergence on local optima rather than the global optimum. Additionally, real-time implementation is restricted by the iterative nature of the solver, which may struggle with highly dynamic or unpredictable environments.
