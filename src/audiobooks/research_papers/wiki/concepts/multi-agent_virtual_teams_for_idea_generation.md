## TLDR

A system where multiple AI agents, modeled after source paper authors, act as heterogeneous perspectives to critique, score, and refine research ideas.

## Body

In the context of research idea generation, a virtual team of agents can be deployed to simulate the collaborative dynamic of human researchers. By modeling these agents after the authors of a source paper, the system leverages heterogeneous perspectives rather than relying on a single, monolithic AI viewpoint.

These agents actively critique, score, and refine proposed ideas through a multi-agent collaborative process. This interactive evaluation functions as a dynamic boundary, continuously steering the search process toward conceptual regions that are structurally sound and highly novel.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

While multi-agent critique improves structural soundness, the agents may eventually converge on narrow technical trajectories or suffer from path-locking if not carefully managed. Furthermore, there is a risk of over-fitting to the simulated personas, which might not perfectly replicate the nuanced, real-world expertise and intuitive leaps of the actual human authors.
