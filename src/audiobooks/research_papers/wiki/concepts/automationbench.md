## TLDR

A benchmark framework designed to evaluate AI agent performance in complex, cross-application enterprise workflows rather than isolated tasks.

## Body

AutomationBench addresses the 'last mile' problem in AI agent development by simulating multi-app environments where coordination is critical. Unlike previous benchmarks that focused on single-app interactions or web browsing, AutomationBench requires agents to navigate entire business workflows involving disparate systems.

The benchmark specifically tests three core capabilities known as the 'trifecta': cross-application coordination, autonomous API discovery, and strict policy adherence. By forcing agents to manage state across various software tools, it aims to bridge the gap between academic research and production-grade automation.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

As a new benchmark, it may face challenges regarding scalability and the complexity of maintaining accurate ground-truth environments for rapidly evolving SaaS APIs. Furthermore, benchmarks that rely heavily on specific tool sets may struggle to generalize if agent architectures become highly specialized for unique enterprise stacks.
