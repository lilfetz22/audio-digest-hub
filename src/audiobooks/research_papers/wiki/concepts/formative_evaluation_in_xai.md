## TLDR

A method of assessing XAI tools based on whether they improve a user's task performance over time, rather than just their understanding of the model.

## Body

Formative evaluation in the context of XAI shifts the success criteria of an explanation. Traditionally, XAI evaluation asks whether a user successfully understood the model's internal mechanics. Formative evaluation instead asks whether the user's performance on the actual task improved over time due to the XAI tool.

This shift aligns with the learner-centric approach, where the XAI system acts as a training wheel or scaffolding mechanism. The ultimate metric of success is human-AI team performance and the human's long-term skill acquisition.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

Measuring long-term task performance improvement is subject to many confounding variables, such as the user's prior experience and external learning. It also requires much longer evaluation cycles compared to simple A/B testing of explanation comprehension.
