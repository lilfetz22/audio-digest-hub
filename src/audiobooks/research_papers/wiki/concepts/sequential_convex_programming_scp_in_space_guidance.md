## TLDR

A numerical optimization technique used as the final safety gatekeeper to ensure trajectories are physically feasible and safe.

## Body

Sequential Convex Programming (SCP) is employed here as the deterministic 'safety gatekeeper' of the system. While the upstream neural models handle the qualitative and geometric aspects of planning, the SCP solver handles the hard constraints of orbital mechanics and safety.

It takes the geometric waypoints generated by the neural network and solves for a trajectory that minimizes fuel consumption while adhering to strict physics-based constraints. This ensures that even if the intent-aligned strategy is theoretically sound, the physical implementation never violates critical safety radii or keep-out zones.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

SCP is computationally expensive and requires precise initialization; if the waypoints provided by the preceding neural network are too far from the feasible manifold, the solver may fail to converge on a valid solution within real-time constraints.
