## TLDR

FELA is a system that automates the cognitive intelligence of data scientists to transform raw logs into predictive features, evolving traditional AutoML into agent-based discovery.

## Body

FELA represents a paradigm shift from traditional AutoML to what is termed "Agent-based Discovery." Instead of merely automating the coding and execution aspects of feature engineering, FELA attempts to automate the cognitive intelligence and domain expertise typically provided by human data scientists.

The system operates by utilizing a hierarchical knowledge base to manage and apply feature engineering logic. By doing so, it effectively converts tribal, unwritten data science knowledge into a scalable and evolving automated system, capable of handling raw, messy log data to consistently extract high-value predictive features without manual intervention.

## Counterarguments / Data Gaps

While FELA automates feature engineering intelligence, relying on a hierarchical knowledge base may require significant initial setup to capture complex "tribal knowledge." Furthermore, fully replacing human intuition in highly novel or poorly understood datasets remains a challenge, and the generated features might still require human validation to ensure they do not introduce data leakage or spurious correlations.
